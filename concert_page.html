TOM

I've been a fan of Maroon 5 for ages, and I was so excited to get the chance to see them! Their opening acts, Neon Trees and Owl City, were great, really amped up the crowd to see the main act. Their lights and backdrop effects were amazing (they had a projector, so the scene changed for each song to fit the mood of it). Adam would tell stories and talk to the crowd in between songs. He even took a woman's camera and took a video! His singing and the rest of the band's performance was incredible. I would definitely go see them again!!

ANGELINA

After the previous show at THE GORGE in George Washington (yes that's the name of a city) was postponed because of high winds that calmed down about 1//2 hr after the show was cancelled, I was fortunate to have tickets in "The Pit". for the Honda Civic Tour. No weather issues in the "Golden State".

Kelly Clarkson was great and a hard act to follow but Maroon 5 was up for the challenge and absolutely rocked the joint. Great sound, great show, lots of eye candy for guys and girls alike. I saw Maroon 5 about 10 years ago during the Songs About Jane tour and neither show was a disappointment. My only complaint is Maroon 5 did not play long enough.But that can be said about any great show, including 3 hours of The Eagles. Can't wait for the next tour.
